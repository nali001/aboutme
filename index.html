About Me

I am currently a Postdoc in the INDElab and the Discovery Lab, working with Prof. Paul Groth. I finished my Ph.D. in the MNS group, supervised by Dr. Zhiming Zhao and Prof. Paola Grosso. My research interests are research asset search, data quality control and active learning.
